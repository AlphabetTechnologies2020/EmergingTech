    What is Edge Computing?

    Edge Computing is a distributed computing foundation that focuses to bring applications and computing as close to the source of the data as possible, rather than depending on a central data location that can be seven seas away.

    Edge Computing Data Handling:

    Edge computing has completely changed the way of data handling, processing, and sending from millions of devices and applications all around the world. This technology allows us to run a few of the processes in the cloud and then moving those to the local servers, systems, and IOT devices.

    Proximity of Client and Server:

    Thankful to this technology as it allows closeness between client and server that helps in providing various strong business benefits, including faster processing, much-improved response time, and much better bandwidth availability. All these benefits sum up to provide:

  • Real-time data without having delayed issues that can affect the performance of devices and applications.
  • Reduces the amount of data processing at the remote location.
  • Cost-saving by doing all processing locally.
