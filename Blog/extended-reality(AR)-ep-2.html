Augmented Reality (AR) An Interactive Experience
(Extended Reality (Episode-2))

Syed Talha Hussain Tech Researcher, Pk Emerging Technologies aims to bring the future technologies to tech savvy audience.


To understand that, Augmented Reality is really an Interactive experience let’s move on and understand, what really an Interactive Experience is:

What is an Interactive Experience?

An Interactive experience is a user experience that engages the attention of user and stop them to focus at what is going on, that would likely helpful for them in a sense that it made easy for them to understand that particular system and its operations.

What is Augmented Reality (AR)?

Augmented reality is an experience that uses existing reality and physical objects to deliver computer generated content that placed over reality. It enables it users by providing appropriate information to its user at the right time.
In essence, AR is a technology that superimpose computer-generated enhancements or images over a user’s view of the physical world. These images usually takes the shapes as Video files, 3D models, and information. It can be called as an Enhanced Version of Reality.

Features of Augmented Reality (AR):

AR is based on three basic features:

  1. A combination of real and virtual worlds,
  2. Real-time interaction, and
  3. Accurate 3D registration of virtual and real objects.

How Augmented Reality (AR) is an Interactive Experience:

Augmented reality is an interactive experience where developers, Engineers, and designers are intended to enhance user experience of physical word using computer-generated perceptual or virtual environment.
This is possibly done by specialists who create inputs that raging from audio to video, then graphics to GPS overlays and more in digital content which responds in real time to change in the user’s environment, generally movement.



As AR is an Interactive Experience for users, big organizations and even small businesses tries to implement AR in customer care sector. They are doing this to make good user interaction for their customers and get successful to earn new customers using this positive, helpful, and advance technology.

How Augmented Reality (AR) Works:

Augmented reality works in combination with digital devices such Smartphones, Tablets, and PCs or Laptops. The device itself contain Sensors, Software Applications, etc. that produces digital display onto Physical real life objects.

Examples of Augmented Reality (AR):

AR can be available in various forms. Its potential in today’s advance world is Endless and it becomes only a matter of time before this technology becomes a part of our daily lives. Now it is being used by various industries.

  1. Use of AR In Gaming Industry:

    2. Games like Pokemon Go uses AR technology that appeals and attract its users by providing the way it combined the real world with favorite Pokemon characters.

  2. Use of AR In Medical Industry:

    3. Medical trainings are now prepared using AR, Some medical institutes uses it to train for various surgeries and live operations without the need of physical human body.

  3. Use of AR In Aviation:

    4. Trainings of aircraft flying done using emulators that uses AR technology. This is very much helpful and save cost and lives.

  4. AR In Media industry:

    5. Media uses AR technology to increase viewer’s attraction. From Sports events to weather broadcast AR becoming more popular in digital media.
