Computer Vision (Episode-1)

Ali Tech Researcher, Pk Emerging Technologies aims to bring the future technologies to tech savvy audience.


Computer Vision, abbreviated as CV, is a field of study that seeks to develop techniques to help computers “see” and understand the content of digital data which could be text in the form of image, or real-time/non-real-time images such as photographs and videos.

We could define the term as the ability of computer algorithms to interpret what a camera observes in the environment. Computer Vision allows AR applications to better lay the digital objects over the real world.

The idea of computer vision is to mimic human vision feature of the brain, however, it largely remains an unsolved problem based both on the limited understanding of biological vision and because of the complexity of vision perception in a dynamic and nearly infinitely varying physical world.

Why humans want computer to see

The internet is comprised of text and images. It is relatively straightforward to index and search text, but in order to index and search images, algorithms need to know what the images contain. For the longest time, the content of images and video has remained opaque, best described using the meta descriptions provided by the person that uploaded them.

Smartphones have cameras, and taking a photo or video and sharing it has never been easier, resulting in the incredible growth of modern social networks like Instagram.

YouTube might be the second largest search engine and hundreds of hours of video are uploaded every minute and billions of videos are watched every day.

To get the most out of image data, we need computers to “see” an image and understand the content.
This is a trivial problem for a human.

  • A person can describe the content of a photograph they have seen once.
  • A person can summarize a video that they have only seen once.
  • A person can recognize a face that they have only seen once before.

    • We require at least the same capabilities from computers in order to unlock the incredible information and details in our images and videos.
