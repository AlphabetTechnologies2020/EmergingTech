IOT A Future of Technology

Faisal Tech Researcher, Pk Emerging Technologies aims to bring the future technologies to tech savvy audience.


IOT in simple words could be understood as When things get connected through a network and then controlled using sensors, actuators, and software to perform certain tasks based on their functionality.

Concept of IOT

Technology breaks its records of advancements day by day and we are well aware of how new technology has transformed our life entirely and add comforts to our lives as we compare it with the past decade or two. Now we can automate our interaction with things using remote access to the things we want to operate no matter we are seven seas away from it just because of the blessings of IOT. We would get many benefits from adding connectivity and intelligence to the physical environment.

Potential of IOT

IOT has enough potential to brighten the future of technology, it leads to building a large number of innovations and business opportunities that are affecting and innovating our day to day life, on a positive note.

Although the internet makes the world a global village, the IOT moves one step ahead and emerges to produces the most dynamic and exciting developments in information and communications technology.

Industries Benefiting Through IOT

The past few years have witnessed the attachment of a much broader range of devices to the network. These have included:

  • Automobiles,
  • Home and Office appliances,
  • Electric meters and controls,
  • Medical tools and machines,
  • Traffic controls,
  • Street lights,
  • Smart TVs,
  • Cloud Computing,
  • Digital assistants such as Google Home and Amazon Alexa etc.

    • Examples of IOT

    Some more elaboration that second above examples, Like:

  • In automobiles, we have GPRS trackers, maintenance, monitoring, and operating using mobile applications.
  • We could automate our home using this IOT technology and control things through remote access like Turning on the air-condition and set the desired temperature, remotely view CCTV camera recording for the security of our house, turning on and off appliances like fan and lights, etc.
  • View, control, and manage our electricity consumption using IOT electric meters.

    • Conclusion

    IOT will be the future of technology as the above-mentioned parameters get advancements day by day and make lives better and better. It is also estimated that there will be more than 21 billion IOT by 2025.
