How Intelligent Apps Contributes Towards Promising Future?

Muhammad Saad Tech Researcher, Pk Emerging Technologies aims to bring the future technologies to tech savvy audience.


It is not wrong when I say that life is incomplete without smartphones. Just imagine the feeling when we left our phone at home in this situation the feeling arrives that we are completely cut off from the world, how we could communicate with our family, how we could work or even how we could work smartly etc. These feelings are just because smartphones or gadgets make our life easy and manageable just by downloading and using awesome-innovative applications in these devices.

Life Transformation Using Intelligent Apps

These intelligent applications set new trends and unlock great opportunities for these smart devices and transform our lives to more productive as we can manage our relations, professional relations, work-life, and activities in an effectual, engaging, and interactive way.

Why They are called Intelligent

Intelligent apps are called intelligent because they are very smart and make our routine tasks and experiences remarkably well. We appreciate these applications and tools, using them we can do shopping, pay utility bills, Online banking, control our home appliances, virtually visit our desired places using (VR glasses), and much more. These intelligent apps take our smartphones to the next level as they can recognize human speech searches on the internet based on spoken words or answer their questions or translate their language.

Intelligent Apps Brings Digital Inovation

Most companies can leverage these apps to bring about digital innovation and deliver the next level of customer experience. Large industries are using intelligent applications that collect and process data from a variety of sources and provide real-time experiences using technologies such as machine learning and artificial intelligence.

As a user you noticed that Facebook and YouTube show data that is relevant to your interest, This is because it reads you search results and understand user requirements, and shows relevant posts, advertisements, or different content. Such types of applications make much smarter use of the device’s feature to dedicatedly deliver highly relevant information and suggestions.

Some Examples of Intelligent Apps

Examples of some intelligent apps that revolutionize our daily lives are as follows:

  • Google Assistant, Alexa, Siri, Amazon,
  • Facebook Application,
  • Polestar’s Android-powered infotainment system integrates with the car’s operating system,
  • Tesla car’s Operating system
  • Ada Health application,
  • Microsoft Pix Application,
  • Google Lens,
  • Security apps like mobile iris scanner and Fingerprint scanner, etc.

    • Conclusion

    As we know we are gradually moving towards a revolutionized world with smart homes, driverless cars, and everything around us turning smart, intelligent apps contribute towards a promising future. We have come a long way from the basic apps that came with the early generations of mobiles. Successful implementation of these intelligent apps grow and evolve alongside technology to ensure high success rates.
